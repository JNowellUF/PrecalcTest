How to factor when the leading coefficient isn’t one.

In the previous section we developed the method of factoring by grouping and the section before that we developed a method of factoring coefficients to factor quadratics with a leading coefficient of one. We can use the same method of factoring coefficients on polynomials that have a leading coefficient that isn’t one, but it gets much more messy and difficult to use. Instead there is a generally easier way to tackle this method that is actually a hybrid of the factoring coefficients and factor by grouping technique, called the “AC Method”.
