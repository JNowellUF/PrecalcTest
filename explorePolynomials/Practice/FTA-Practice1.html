The Fundamental Theorem of Algebra says that the number of zeros is exactly equal to the degree of the polynomial.
1.1 : There are at least exactly at most real-valued solutions.
Although we know there are exactly the same number of solutions as the degree of the polynomial, some of them might be complex-valued.
