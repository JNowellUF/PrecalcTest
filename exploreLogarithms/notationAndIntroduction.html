Logarithms are perhaps the most artificial feeling function in our library of functions. If we remember, exponential functions were one-to-one (passing the vertical and horizontal line tests) and are thus invertible. Logarithms are precisely the inverse functions that correspond to the exponential functions of our last topic.

Notation and how to avoid it!

It turns out that logarithms are extremely useful in many unexpected ways.
