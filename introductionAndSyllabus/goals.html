This is the introduction to the overall course and it contains the syllabus as well as grade information.

By the end of this section students should be able to:
  • Know the goals of the course.
  • Understand what kinds of things to expect in this course.
  • Be familiar with the Contextual Based Learning approach.
  • Find the information they need that is contained in the syllabus.
  • Know how grades are calculated for this course, and calculate their own grades at any point in the semester.
